Planet EARTH

Know about Our Home "Planet-EARTH"

Our home planet Earth is a rocky, terrestrial planet. It has a solid and active surface with mountains, valleys, canyons, plains and so much more.
Earth is special because it is an ocean planet. Water covers 70% of Earth's surface. Earth is also known as Blue Planet,Due to its abundant water and the appearance of its surface from space.

Earth's Atmosphere

Our atmosphere is made mostly of nitrogen and has plenty of oxygen for us to breathe.
The atmosphere also protects us from incoming meteoroids, most of which break up in our atmosphere
they can strike the surface as meteorites.

Composition of the Atmosphere:
Nitrogen (N2): ~78%
Oxygen (O2): ~21%
Argon (Ar): ~0.93%
Carbon Dioxide (CO2): ~0.04%
Trace Gases: Includes neon, helium, methane, krypton, hydrogen, and ozone.
Water Vapor: Varies from 0% to 4%,
depending on location and weather conditions.

Overview

Overview of Earth Planet Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known celestial body to support life.
It is unique in our solar system due to its water-rich environment and diverse biosphere. Earth is characterized by its various spheres that interact to create a habitable environment.

Importance of Earth

Biodiversity: Home to millions of species of plants, animals, and microorganisms.
Resources: Provides natural resources such as water, minerals, and fossil fuels.
System: temperature and weather patterns, supporting life.
